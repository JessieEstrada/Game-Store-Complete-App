New 2023 Game Releases

Best releases this year!

Aliens Invading 5

Step into a retro-futuristic pixelated world in "Aliens Invading 5," the latest installment of the classic arcade-inspired action game series. The galaxy is once again under siege by extraterrestrial forces, and it's up to you, the valiant space defender, to save humanity from the impending invasion.

Fruit Attack

Welcome to a whimsical and colorful world of fruity fun in "Fruit Attack"! Play as a lovable character on a mission to defend their fruit-filled paradise from mischievous, rascally critters who just can't resist snatching those delicious fruits.

Undead Odyssey

Embark on a thrilling journey in Undead Odyssey, a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a zombie outbreak. As a lone survivor, you must navigate through the decaying wastelands, battling hordes of the undead, and uncovering the mysteries behind the cataclysmic event.

DinoSoul: Guardians of the Ancient Beasts

Welcome to a world where ancient dinosaurs roam the untamed lands, and the art of DinoSoul taming holds the key to power and survival. You will embark on an epic adventure filled with danger, wonder, and the bond between humans and these majestic prehistoric creatures.
